Post-deal Projects

Integration strategy

OTC Pharmaceutical and Consumer Goods - Russia

QUESTION

  • A British Pharmaceutical and Consumer goods company needed to merge its operations in Russia with a newly acquired Russian FMCG company
  • Complexity: 2 incompatible business models (go-to-market, sales operations, logistics, incentive systems, and culture), the risk of destroying up to 50% of the revenue, time pressure due to unsuccessful previous attempts, very low collaboration from the acquire company

RESULT

  • A hybrid business model respecting the business fundamentals of the acquirer
  • An ambitious 2-year integration of the companies together with a transformation plan (inc. digitalisation of field sales forces operations, carve-out of non-strategic activities and closure of non-strategic logistic capabilities…)

APPROACH

  • Description of both businesses: clients, products, operating models (e.g. sales KPIs, processes, organisation…), capabilities and financials
  • Description of the market perspectives and business priorities for each company stand-alone
  • Development of a strategy for the merger: vision, strategy, business, and operating model
  • Detailed financial modelling and simulation of various options
  • Multiple workshops with the board of management and CEO
  • Very detailed description of the target merged company (financials, improvement levers and impacts, sales go-to-market and operating model, logistic model, IT tools, incentive systems, support function organisation…)
  • Detailed transition and implementation plan

CLIENT FEEDBACK

“You have led the biggest merger for the firm for the last 20 years and clearly helped us clarify the what, why, and how. You have been instrumental in supporting our decision to move forward with an ambitious integration plan that delivered beyond our initial financial expectation”

Head of Strategy – Global FMCG Company

Integration execution

OTC Pharmaceutical and Consumer Goods - Russia

QUESTION

  • A British Pharmaceutical and Consumer goods company needed some support to deliver the integration execution of a recent acquisition
  • Complexity: to not hinder the business results, tight integration deadline

RESULT

  • +60% revenue delivered in 3 years reaching £0.7Bn

APPROACH

  • Development of a detailed operations design for sales, marketing, logistics, support functions (capabilities, processes, skills…)
  • Organisation of the integration team and capabilities including people and tools
  • Development of the program management (400 main actions and 11 workstreams), coordination and follow up
  • Support provided to each workgroup until completion
  • Development and implementation of pilots for the most complex operations before full-scale rollout
  • Regular reporting to the board on the Integration progress and its business impacts
  • Support to change management

CLIENT FEEDBACK

“Thank you for your hard work, relentless energy, and the structure you brought. Without you, we wouldn’t have made it”

CEO Russia CIS – Global FMCG company

Carve out

UK Medical products company - Russia

QUESTION

  • A British global company producing and delivering FMCG, and medical products wanted to carve-out their medical business in Russia

RESULT

  • The carve-out was successfully executed with the carved-out company sold

APPROACH

  • Description of the company’s activity segments, capabilities and assets
  • Analyses to define the exit strategy options including potential investors and buyers
  • Identification of shared capabilities and assets, together with and assessment of the level and nature of entanglement
  • Allocation of shared capabilities and assets
  • Evaluation of solutions for carved-out business to ensure continued operations
  • Calculation of de-synergies – impacts on the business, P&L, and balance sheet
  • Calculation of separation costs both on-going and one-off
  • Identification and calculation of growth areas for the carved-out company to build a compelling investment story
  • Development of an implementation plan

CLIENT FEEDBACK

“Your structured approach and analyses helped us to decide and execute the carve-out and deliver the exit strategy successfully”

VP Medical products - UK
