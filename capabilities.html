Strategy

In a context where information is plethoric, it becomes increasingly critical to provide deciders with clarity in order to develop the vision and direction for the company.

We offer a comprehensive understanding of market dynamics, encompassing clients' needs, value space map, competitive business models, key success factors, differentiating factors, and emerging trends. We identify, describe, and evaluate the most favourable strategic scenarios considering the company's assets.

Once the vision is determined and aligned with the company's overarching objectives and financial plan, we craft a strategic plan that not only defines the direction and objectives but also acts as a guiding framework for each business unit and department within the organisation.

M&A Pre-Deal

  • Target screening
  • Acquisition due diligence
  • Vendor due diligence

To effectively assess the validity and viability of an acquisition, understanding the target company's sustainable value creation, revenue potential, and potential business synergies is essential.

Key analyses establish confidence in the decision-making process. They include market evaluation, competitor analysis, identification of critical success factors, strengths and weaknesses assessment, culture fit evaluation, and identification of key talents.

We assist our clients in evaluating target companies as investment opportunities, providing regular updates, and openly discussing findings with the Investment Board to build a comprehensive understanding of the opportunity's value and risks.

M&A Post-Deal

  • Business & operating model
  • Implementation roadmap
  • Integration implementation

Transactions is one of the most powerful business initiatives to create value. Yet over 70% of buyers do not materialise the synergies planned pre⁠-⁠acquisition.

A few reasons for this:

  • Over optimistic business assessment when acquiring
  • Overlooked business impacts of intangible factors
  • Poor integration execution
  • Underestimated integration time and on-off costs

We have helped global companies to deliver the integration of their acquisitions in difficult cases and situations yet over delivering against their initial plan in terms of actual integration time, synergies materialised and overall Newco performance.

Transformation

  • Turnaround
  • Digital transformation / AI
  • Efficiency

Consequence of a significant change in a company's direction or a simple performance enhancement, companies are increasingly required to undergo transformations using various levers such as business model changes, business process reengineering (BPR), automation, management system changes, skills enhancement, and culture shift.

Today, Data and Artificial Intelligence (AI) is emerging as a new competitive tool. At the crossroad of data, algorithms, business, and IT, it requires a clear digital and AI strategy, as well as significant changes in terms of organization, culture, operations, skillsets, and ultimately, data strategy.

We assist our clients to achieve their company's full potential by leveraging our expertise in business transformation, Data & AI strategy, and project implementation.

Marketing

  • Offer design & positioning
  • Pricing

Strategic marketing focuses on identifying client needs and developing a value proposition to meet them. It involves determining what clients are willing to pay for and defining the corresponding offer and pricing.

We assist in defining our clients' marketing strategy: clients’ needs, value proposition, offer positioning and features, revenue model, pricing strategy and calculation, USP for communication.

Beyond the technical aspects, we place specific emphasis on:

  • The thorough understanding of what the client really values and why
  • The alignment of the financials of the offer with the actual level of market appetite
  • The reality and significance of the offer’s differentiation
