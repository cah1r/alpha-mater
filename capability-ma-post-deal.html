Transactions is one of the most powerful business initiatives to create value. Yet over 70% of buyers do not materialise the synergies planned pre⁠-⁠acquisition.

A few reasons for this include:

  • Over optimistic business assessment when acquiring
  • Overlooking business impacts of intangible factors
  • Poor integration execution
  • Underestimated integration time and on-off costs

We help global companies to deliver the integration of their acquisitions in difficult cases and situations yet over delivering against their initial plan in terms of actual integration time, synergies materialised and overall Newco performance.
