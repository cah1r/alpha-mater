M&A Pre-Deal

  • Target screening
  • Acquisition due diligence
  • Vendor due diligence

To effectively assess the validity of an acquisition, it is essential to understand the value that the target company creates sustainably, its future revenue potential, and the potential business synergies with the acquirer.

Key analyses establish confidence in the decision-making process. These include market evaluation, competitor analysis, identification of critical success factors, assessment of strengths and weaknesses, evaluation of cultural fit, and identification of key talents.

We assist our clients in evaluating target companies as investment opportunities, providing regular updates, and openly discussing our findings with the Investment Committee to develop a comprehensive understanding of the opportunity's value and associated risks.
